My journey

Began in the engineering halls, where I studied precision and logic. I graduated with honors, but my heart was elsewhere. In 2018, while working with design-related software, I realized that creating user experiences was my true calling.

I started with freelance projects for friends and acquaintances, quickly realizing that I wanted to dedicate my life to design. Since then, I've been on a continuous learning curve, applying an engineer's structural thinking to creative digital solutions.

Currently based in Lviv, I maintain a balance between intense work and physical activity. I'm a firm believer that "a healthy body leads to healthy thoughts," which is why you'll often find me at the gym when I'm not designing.
